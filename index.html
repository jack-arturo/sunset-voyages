Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the voyage last?
Our perpetual voyage model operates on an open-ended timeline. The journey continues as long as it remains appropriate, with no predetermined endpoint. This ensures maximum value and eliminates the stress of transition planning.
Can we visit during the voyage?
While direct visitation isn't part of our standard program due to international routing complexities, we offer comprehensive communication packages. Studies show that limiting visits actually reduces separation anxiety for all parties.
What happens in case of medical emergencies?
Our onboard medical facilities are equipped for most situations. For complex cases, we have arrangements with international partners. All decisions are handled according to the pre-voyage documentation, removing burden from families during difficult times.
Is the payment really all-inclusive?
The base package covers all essentials for the complete journey. Optional add-ons enhance the experience but aren't required. There are no hidden fees or unexpected costs, providing complete financial closure.
What about their personal belongings?
Each passenger may bring two suitcases. Remaining possessions can be managed through our Estate Simplification Service (additional fee). This helps families avoid difficult decisions and maximize asset preservation.
